Transcribe is an app that leverages high-precision AI technology to quickly and accurately convert speech into text. It allows you to easily manage and share audio data from meetings, interviews, lectures, notes, and more.

Key Features

Real-Time Transcription: Converts speech to text in real-time and displays it instantly.
High-Accuracy Speech Recognition: Utilizes the latest AI technology to achieve high-accuracy speech recognition.
Multilingual Support: Supports multiple languages including Japanese, English, Chinese, and many others.
Recommended For

Business professionals looking to efficiently record meetings and interviews.
Students who want to easily record lecture and seminar content.
Anyone who wants to save the hassle of taking notes.
With Simple Transcribe, effortlessly convert speech to text and enhance your daily tasks!
